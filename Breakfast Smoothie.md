# Breakfast Smoothie
#### 1 servings - 500 cal
## Ingredients
-   1 banana
-   2 tbsp smooth peanut butter
-   1 cup almond milk
-   2 scoops organic protein powder

## Directions
1.  Place everything in the blender and blend until smooth.
