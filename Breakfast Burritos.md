# Breakfast Burritos
## Ingredients
-   1 gluten free spinach wrap
-   2/3 cup liquid egg whites
-   1/2 cup diced canned tomato
-   1/2 cup ground beef 85%

## Directions
1.  Brown beef in skillet
2.  Cook spinach and tomato in skillet
3.  Place in wrap and top with Sriracha
